## Instructions

1. Blend all the base ingredients into a smooth base and freeze flat for at least 16 hours.
2. Make the ganache: stir the unsweetened cocoa powder into the sugar-free syrup until smooth (about 15 g total).
3. Run the pint under hot water for at least 60 seconds.
4. Process on the Lite Ice Cream setting, and respin if it comes out very powdery.
5. Make a hole down to the bottom with a butter knife and fill it with the ganache and half of the cookie dough bites.
6. Run the Mix-In cycle.
7. Top with the remaining cookie dough bites and serve.
