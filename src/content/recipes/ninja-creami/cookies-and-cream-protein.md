## Instructions

1. Add the protein shake, vanilla protein powder, sea salt, and any sweetener to a Creami pint container and whisk or blend until smooth, with no lumps of protein powder.
2. Freeze on a level surface for 24 hours.
3. Process on the Lite Ice Cream / Protein Ice Cream setting.
4. If it comes out crumbly or powdery, add a splash of milk and re-spin until creamy.
5. Add chocolate chips to the center and run the Mix-In setting.
