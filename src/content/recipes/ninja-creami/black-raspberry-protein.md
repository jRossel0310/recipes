## Instructions

1. Blend all ingredients into a smooth base and freeze flat for at least 16 hours.
2. Run the pint under hot water for at least 60 seconds.
3. Process on the Lite Ice Cream setting.
4. Run the Respin setting until it reaches your desired consistency.
