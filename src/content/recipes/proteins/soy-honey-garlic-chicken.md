## Instructions

1. Whisk together all the sauce ingredients (everything except the chicken, oil, and cornstarch). If using cornstarch, stir it into the cold water separately.
2. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high. Cook the chicken strips in a single layer, turning, 4-6 minutes until browned and cooked through.
3. Pour in the sauce, toss to coat, and simmer 1-2 minutes. If using the slurry, add it now and cook about 30 seconds until glossy.
4. Serve over rice or alongside vegetables.

## Notes

* Fresh garlic and ginger are brighter; the powders keep it pantry-friendly.
