## Instructions

1. Rub the kosher salt into the flesh side of the salmon only. Refrigerate 30 minutes to 1 hour.
2. Rinse or wipe off the excess cure and pat the salmon very dry.
3. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
4. Stir together the olive oil, garlic, lemon zest, Dijon, and herbs, then spread over the flesh side.
5. Place skin-side down on a parchment-lined or lightly oiled tray.
6. Roast 10-14 minutes depending on thickness, pulling it at 114°F internal with the center still slightly translucent.
7. Rest 3-5 minutes.
8. Finish with a squeeze of lemon, flaky salt, and extra herbs.

## Notes

* The cure is light; much past an hour and the texture starts heading toward gravlax.
* When scaling, adjust the roasting time by thickness, not weight.
