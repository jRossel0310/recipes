## Instructions

1. Slice the peppers and onion into strips about ¼-inch thick.
2. Heat the avocado oil in a large cast-iron skillet over medium-high until sizzling hot.
3. Add the peppers, onions, and all the seasonings. Cook 7-8 minutes, stirring occasionally; let them sit a minute or two between stirs if you want more char.
4. In the last minute, add the garlic to an open spot in the pan and cook 30 seconds.
5. Stir everything together, scraping up the browned bits, and serve.

## Notes

* Add sliced jalapeños or serranos (seeds in) for heat.
* Cast iron or stainless gets you the charred edges; non-stick won't.
* Keeps about 5 days in the fridge.
