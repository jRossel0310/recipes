## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C) and line a large baking sheet with parchment.
2. Mix the oil, soy sauce, and all the spices in a large bowl into a paste.
3. Crumble the tofu into the bowl with your fingers and toss until coated.
4. Spread in a single layer on the baking sheet. Bake 30-35 minutes, stirring every 10 minutes, until nicely browned. Watch them toward the end; the small bits darken first.
5. Transfer to a skillet with ¾ cup salsa and heat through. Add more salsa if you like it saucier.
6. Serve in taco shells or tortillas with your usual toppings.

## Notes

* Don't press the tofu; the extra moisture helps the crumbles soak up the salsa.
* Make ahead: keep the baked crumbles dry in the fridge (3-4 days) and add salsa when you reheat.
