## Instructions

**Make the sauce:**

1. Toast the dried chiles 2-3 minutes in a dry pan.
2. Soak them 15 minutes in hot water; reserve the soaking liquid.
3. Blend the chiles with garlic, cumin, oregano, and about 2 cups of the soaking liquid.
4. Strain and set aside.

**Make the filling:**

5. Boil the carrots until tender; drain.
6. Sauté the mushrooms until deeply browned; set aside.
7. Sauté the onions and garlic until soft.
8. Add the drained black beans; season with cumin, oregano, salt, and chili powder.
9. Cook the beans 5-8 minutes until slightly dry.
10. Lightly mash about a quarter of the beans to create a creamy binder.
11. Combine the carrots, mushrooms, onions, and beans.
12. Optionally stir in 1-2 cups of enchilada sauce for added moisture and flavor.

**Assemble:**

13. Warm the tortillas; fill and roll.
14. Place seam-side down in pans.
15. Cover with the sauce and vegan cheese.
16. Bake at 350°F for 25-30 minutes.

## Notes

* The source does not quantify the sauce chiles - use a handful of dried chiles to taste.
