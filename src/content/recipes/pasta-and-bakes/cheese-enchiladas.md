## Instructions

1. Heat the oil in a skillet over medium.
2. Soften each tortilla briefly in the oil; drain.
3. Fill each tortilla with the cheese mixture and roll tightly.
4. Place seam-side down in pans.
5. Cover with enchilada sauce and the remaining cheese.
6. Bake at 350°F for 15-20 minutes until bubbly.

## Notes

* Combine the cheddar and Queso Oaxaca, reserving some to top the enchiladas before baking.
* The vegetarian option for the dinner.
* Nutrition is a rough estimate - verify and adjust.
