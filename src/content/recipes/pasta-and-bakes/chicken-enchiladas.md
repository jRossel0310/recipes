## Instructions

1. Mix all of the spice ingredients in a large bowl.
2. Season the chicken with half the spice mix; set aside.
3. Heat oil on the stovetop; cook the chicken 6-8 minutes per side until it reaches 165°F internal.
4. Remove the chicken and shred.
5. In a wok or large pan, sauté the onions, jalapeños, and garlic for 5-6 minutes.
6. Add the tomatoes and the remaining spice mix; cook 3-4 minutes.
7. Combine the shredded chicken with the vegetable mixture.
8. Warm the tortillas; fill, roll, and place seam-side down in pans.
9. Cover with enchilada sauce and melting cheese.
10. Bake at 350°F for 20-25 minutes until the cheese is melted and the edges are golden.

## Notes

* Scaled for the co-op: about 120 servings (~2 enchiladas each).
* Cook the chicken in batches so the pan is never crowded.
* The reference doc lists "cover with sauce" but does not quantify it; ~8-10 cans of enchilada sauce is an estimate here - adjust for full coverage.
* Nutrition is a rough estimate - verify and adjust.
