## Instructions

1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta until al dente according to the package directions. Reserve a little pasta water, then drain.
2. While the pasta cooks, add the minced garlic and milk to a large pan. Warm gently over medium-low heat, stirring often, without letting the milk boil hard.
3. Add the light cream cheese, salt, black pepper, smoked paprika, and nutmeg. Whisk until the cream cheese has melted and the sauce is smooth.
4. Reduce the heat to low and add the grated cheddar. Stir until melted.
5. Add the drained pasta and stir until evenly coated. If the sauce is too thick, loosen it with a small splash of reserved pasta water. Taste and adjust the salt before serving.

## Notes

* Keep the heat low after adding the cheeses to prevent the sauce from splitting.
* Nutmeg deepens the flavor of the cheese sauce without making it taste sweet.
