## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
2. Mix together all crust ingredients until combined.
3. Press the ground chicken mix onto a parchment-lined baking sheet into a thin pizza shape.
4. Bake for 25 minutes, or until golden and firm.
5. Turn oven up to 425°F and add sauce, cheese, and toppings.
6. Bake another 5-10 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Broil 1-2 minutes at the end if you want it crispier.
7. Finish with the honey and sriracha.
