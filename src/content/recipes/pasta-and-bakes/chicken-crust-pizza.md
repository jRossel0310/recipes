## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
2. Mix together all crust ingredients until combined.
3. Press the ground chicken mix onto a parchment-lined baking sheet into a thin pizza shape.
4. Bake for 25 minutes, or until golden and firm.
5. Turn oven up to 425°F and add sauce, cheese, and toppings.
6. Bake another 5–10 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
7. Optional: broil for 1–2 minutes for extra crispiness.

## Notes

* Top it off with a light drizzle of honey and hot sauce (sriracha) for a sweet and spicy kick.
