## Instructions

1. Toss everything except the lemon juice together.
2. Roast at 425°F for 30–40 minutes, flipping once halfway through, until browned and crisp.
3. When they come out, add the lemon juice and toss again.

## Notes

* The lemon and herbs will match the salmon without making the whole meal taste overly citrusy.
