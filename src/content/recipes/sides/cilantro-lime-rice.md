## Instructions

1. Add the rice, chicken broth, salt, and butter (if using) to the rice cooker.
2. Cook using the normal white rice setting.
3. When the cycle finishes, leave the lid closed and let the rice rest for 10 minutes.
4. Fluff with a fork, then stir in the lime zest, lime juice, and chopped cilantro.

## Notes

* Keep the lid closed during the 10-minute rest so the rice finishes steaming evenly.
* Add the lime and cilantro after cooking to keep their flavors fresh.
