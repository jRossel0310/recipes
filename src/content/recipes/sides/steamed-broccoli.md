## Instructions

1. Add about 1 inch of water to a pot and bring it to a simmer. Place the broccoli in a steamer basket above the water and cover.
2. Steam for 5–7 minutes, until bright green and tender to your liking.
3. Season with salt, pepper, and butter or olive oil if desired. Serve on the side or mix into the finished pasta.

## Notes

* Check early to avoid overcooking; the broccoli should remain bright green.
