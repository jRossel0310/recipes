## Instructions

1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Add the zucchini in a single layer and season with Lawry's Seasoned Salt.
3. Cook for 3–4 minutes without moving it much, then stir and cook for another 4–6 minutes until browned and tender.

## Notes

* Do not crowd the skillet; cook in batches if needed to keep the zucchini from steaming.
