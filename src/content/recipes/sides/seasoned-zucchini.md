## Instructions

1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Add the zucchini in a single layer (batches if needed) and season with Lawry's Seasoned Salt.
3. Cook 3-4 minutes without moving it much, then stir and cook another 4-6 minutes until browned and tender.
