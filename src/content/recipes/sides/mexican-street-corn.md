## Instructions

1. Heat a cast iron skillet over high heat. Cook the drained corn 8-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until lightly charred.
2. Off the heat, stir in the butter and a pinch of salt.
3. Transfer to a bowl and stir in the mayonnaise, lime juice, and 1 teaspoon of the chili powder.
4. Top with cotija, the remaining chili powder, and cilantro.
5. Serve warm or at room temperature.

## Notes

* Fresh corn works too: grill husked cobs until charred, cut off the kernels, and proceed.
* No cotija? Feta, queso fresco, or Parmesan all work.
* Keeps 3 days in the fridge; a splash of lime juice freshens up leftovers.
